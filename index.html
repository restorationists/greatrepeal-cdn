The Great Repeal

A set of seven open source UK parliamentary bills published under Creative Commons (CC BY 4.0) licence for anyone to pass in a single day. They reset a century of legislative slop to restore the natural negative rights and liberty of the individual, allowing them to go about their life without unnecessary interference from the British state.

The RoBO Act and Free Speech Act have been released, as has a guide to BINCA.

We're finalising the rest. The date is symbolic.
